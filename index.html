About

After graduating University of Toronto, I took Interactive Media Management program at George Brown College where I learned how to design interactive media. By combining my development skills I learned from University and Bitmaker Lab, I was able to create websites of my own.

I try my best to create unique and innovative website that fits the needs by investigating interaction between user and the goal. I strongly think that the future of the website will not simply be in the monitor nor cell phone but in combination with other technologies such as near-field communication, augmented reality and virtual reality.
